Farrow Ulven is a philosopher specializing in social and political philosophy with broader interests in the history of philosophy, metaphysics, jurisprudence, philosophy of language, and philosophy of science. Farrow completed a B.A. in philosophy at University of Minnesota and is currently pursuing a doctorate in philosophy at University of Iowa.

Farrow is the founder of a non-profit organization furthering philosophical and scientific research aimed at developing insight and solutions to pressing environmental and social issues. Read more about the organization at Common Nature Institute.
